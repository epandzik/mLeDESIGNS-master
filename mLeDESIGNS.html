m.L.e. DESIGNS is a single woman design company. I started doing freelance graphic design work while living in Portland, Oregon and have continued after I moved back to my hometown of Kansas City, Missouri. I’ve done work for various start-ups and small businesses, as well as in-house design for larger hospitals and software companies. I’ve a bit of experience with advertising as well.

Interestingly enough, I still haven’t decided which of these — advertising, in-house and freelance — is my favorite work environment. They’ve all got pros and cons. I think that’s part of the reason I’ve been working to broaden my skills.

Currently I’m trying to better understand web development and on advancing my coding expertise. I’ve built this site, created all the images and animations used. I am working on making it responsive across multiple devices/clients. The more I know, the more I realize that’s going to be a continuous battle.

At this point, I work full-time in-house, so my availability for freelance work is somewhat limited. I enjoy the challenge of taking on a new project, though, so don’t let my hours discourage you. If you like what you see and want to know how I can help you develop a project, please contact me.

If you’d like a peek behind the curtains to me as a person, feel free to take a look at my blog. It holds projects I’m working on, solutions I’ve found (or not) and various other ramblings.
